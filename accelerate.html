Launching Census Accelerate

Census Accelerate pairs creative agencies and community-based organizations to generate innovative content and campaigns reaching hard-to-count populations for the 2020 Census.

Background

The Decennial Census is the largest peacetime mobilization in the United States and one of the most consequential pillars of U.S. democracy. It determines congressional redistricting and apportionment, the distribution of $675B of federal funding, and the formation of countless decisions for policy, business, and planning. The stakes are high: - the effects of the 2020 Census will be felt for at least a decade. In the face of ever-expanding challenges and opportunities, our mandate is to ensure that everyone is counted once, only once, and in the right place.

But there are serious challenges ahead:

  • Rapidly declining response rates, especially within Hard-to-Count populations such as children under five, urban and rural low-income households, households with poor internet access, and others
  • Decreased trust in the government
  • Increased social polarization
  • Minimal quantity of up-to-date and accurate online content
  • The escalating volume of disinformation
  • The rising cost of attention
